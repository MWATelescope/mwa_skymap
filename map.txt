def mwa_apng_adaptive(outfile=None,
                      obsinfo_list=None,
                      max_frame_rate=500,   # Maximum number of milliseconds per frame
                      frame_speed=100,  # Number of milliseconds of video per minute of actual observing time
                      gleamsources=False,
                      plot_text_template=DEFAULT_PLOT_TEXT,
                      inverse=False,
                      background='black',
                      hidenulls=False,
                      beam_type='HBA',
                      plotsize=DEFAULT_PLOTSIZE):
    Produce an animated PNG file, where every <frame_speed>ms of viewing time represents 60 seconds of observing time.

    Frames are unequal in length, each frame generated after <max_frame_rate>ms of video play time, or when the next
    observation starts, whichever occurs first.

    The observations are passed in as an obsinfo_list, and the frames are generated with repeated calls to plot_MWA_obs_frame()
